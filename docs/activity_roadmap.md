## 🛠️ Detailed Activity Breakdown

### 1. Climate Data Hacking Workshop
- **Objective:** Teach students to utilize open-source climate data for agricultural risk prediction.
- **Curriculum:**
    - Intro to Python for Data Science (Jupyter Notebooks).
    - Scraping/API extraction from Weather stations.
    - Analyzing 10-year climate trends in Eastern Thailand.
- **Outcome:** A predictive dashboard showing rainfall trends.

### 2. Deep Dive Lab: CNN for Plant Disease
- **Objective:** Implement Computer Vision to identify durian diseases (e.g., Phytophthora).
- **Curriculum:**
    - Fundamentals of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN).
    - Image Data Augmentation techniques.
    - Training models on local crop disease datasets.
- **Outcome:** Mobile app prototype for real-time disease detection.

### 3. AI-IoT Prototyping Workshop
- **Objective:** Bridge the gap between software (AI) and the physical orchard (IoT).
- **Curriculum:**
    - Wiring ESP32 with Soil Moisture & NPK sensors.
    - Sending data to MQTT/Firebase.
    - Integrating AI prediction logic with sensor triggers.
- **Outcome:** A working "Smart Orchard" node powered by solar energy.

### 4. RBRU-Praneet Tech Showcase 2026
- **Objective:** Demonstrate prototypes to stakeholders and the public.
- **Format:**
    - Innovation Pitching Contest (Students).
    - Tech Booths for local farmers to try the prototypes.
    - Panel discussion: "The Future of AI in Thai Orchards".
- **Outcome:** 5+ high-potential agricultural startups/projects.
